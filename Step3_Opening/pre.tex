\section{Main Problem and Previous Literature}

\frame{
\frametitle{Main Problem}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item How best can investors, auditors, financial analysts, and regultors detect misstatements? 
		\item The paper examine the characteristics of misstating firms along five dimensions: accrual quality, financial performance, non-financial measures, off-balance sheet activities, and market-based measures. 
	\end{itemize}
		\begin{block} \
		"I believe that machine learning (ML) will have a dramatic impact on the field of economics within a short time frame"\         -Susan Athey\cite{Susan2018The}
		\end{block}
	\initclock\quad\cronominutes\pdfcolon\cronoseconds
}

\frame{
\frametitle{Previous Literature}
	\begin{itemize}	
		\item Bell and Carcello\cite{Bell2000A} uses a sample of 77 fraud engagements and 305 nonfraud engagements to estimate the likelihood of fraudulent financial report. The significant risk factors: weak internal control environment, rapid company growth, inadequate or inconsistent relative profitability.
		\item Dechow and Ge\cite{Dechow2011Predicting} examine the 2190 AAERs released between 1982 to 2005 and develop a model to predict misstatements.Their model's precision is 73.8\% and its accuracy is 61.7\%
		\item Period, Bowen and\cite{Perols2015Finding} use a dataset with 51 fraud firms, 15,934 non-fraud firm years, and 109 explanatory variables from prior research. 
    \end{itemize}
}

\frame{
	\frametitle{Tools}
	\begin{itemize}
	\item Combat the imbalanced dataset (data rarity->overfitting)
		\begin{itemize}
		\item Use SMOTE(Synthetic Minority Oversampling Technique)\cite{Chawla2002SMOTE}
 		%由于随机过采样采取简单复制样本的策略来增加少数类样本，这样容易产生模型过拟合的问题，即使得模型学习到的信息过于特别(Specific)而不够泛化(General)SMOTE算法的基本思想是对少数类样本进行分析并根据少数类样本人工合成新样本添加到数据集中
 		\item The previous literature use undersampling method.
 		\end{itemize}
	\item Classifications
		\begin{itemize}
		\item Logit Model
		\item C4.5 (Can deal with Continuous variables)
